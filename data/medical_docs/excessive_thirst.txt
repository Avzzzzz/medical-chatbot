Excessive thirst is a common medical symptom that can be caused by a variety of conditions.

What causes excessive thirst?
- Eating spicy or salty foods.
- Drinking too much caffeine or alcohol.
- Exercising and sweating profusely.
- Feeling sick, with symptoms such as fever, diarrhea and vomiting.
- Pregnancy.
- Certain prescription medications.

When should you see a doctor?
- If excessive thirst is persistent, severe, or worsening
- If it is accompanied by other symptoms like fever, fatigue, or bleeding
- If it interferes with daily life or causes concern

How is excessive thirst treated?
- Treatment depends on the underlying cause
- Over-the-counter medications, lifestyle changes, or prescription treatment may be used
