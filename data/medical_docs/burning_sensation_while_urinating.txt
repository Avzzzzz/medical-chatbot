Burning sensation while urinating(Dysuria) is a common medical symptom that can be caused by a variety of conditions.

What causes burning sensation while urinating?
- Bladder infection (cystitis).
- Urinary tract infection.
- Inflammation of the bladder.

When should you see a doctor?
- If burning sensation while urinating is persistent, severe, or worsening
- If it is accompanied by other symptoms like fever, fatigue, or bleeding
- If it interferes with daily life or causes concern

How is burning sensation while urinating treated?
- Treatment depends on the underlying cause
- Over-the-counter medications, lifestyle changes, or prescription treatment may be used

Which specialist should you consult?
- Urologist
