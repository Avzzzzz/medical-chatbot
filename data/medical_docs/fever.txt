Fever is a common medical symptom that can be caused by a variety of conditions.

What causes fever?
- Bacterial infections.
- Viral infections such as influenza or COVID-19.
- Gastrointestinal (GI) infections.
- Urinary tract infections.
- Skin infections.

When should you see a doctor?
- If fever is persistent, severe, or worsening
- If it is accompanied by other symptoms like fever, fatigue, or bleeding
- If it interferes with daily life or causes concern

How is fever treated?
- Treatment depends on the underlying cause
- Over-the-counter medications, lifestyle changes, or prescription treatment may be used

Which specialist should you consult?
- General Physician
