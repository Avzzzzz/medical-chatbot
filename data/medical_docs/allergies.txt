Allergies is a common medical symptom that can be caused by a variety of conditions.

What causes allergies?
- Allergies happen when your immune system mistakenly identifies foreign proteins — in pollen, pet dander, medications,food or other substances — as something harmful (as if it were a virus or bacteria)
- Inhaling, eating or touching something you’re allergic to activates mechanisms meant to protect you and flush the allergen out of your system
- [Cause 3]

When should you see a doctor?
- If allergies is persistent, severe, or worsening
- If it is accompanied by other symptoms like fever, fatigue, or bleeding
- If it interferes with daily life or causes concern

How is allergies treated?
- Treatment depends on the underlying cause
- Over-the-counter medications, lifestyle changes, or prescription treatment may be used
